Activities

Crazy Wheels

Age:18+

Price Range:From $65

Crazy wheels offers a fun ride on four wheelers through the countryside of La Romana. They offer hotel pick up and drop off for your convenience. You will visit sugar cane fields and ride through the town of the locals. Your tastebuds will also have fun as you sample cacao, coffee and sugar cane straight from the land. Mmm...cafecito with cana.



Saona Island

Age:Any Age

Price Range:From:$59

"En el mar, la vida es mas sabrosa." Life is truly better at the sea. Embark on a 45 minute boat ride to the beautful Saona Island. You will be picked up and dropped off from your hotel so you do not have to worry about driving. In fact, you don't even have to worry about lunch because that is covered as well! Saona Island flaunts its crystal clear water,white sand and relaxing ambience. You can spend all day in the water or by the sand enjoying your favorite book. Get your swimsuit ready, pack your sunscreen, and make sure to leave all your stress behind.





Catalina Island Snorkeling



Age:6+

Price:From:$48 to $110

This is for all the aquatic life lovers! Join an experienced guide as you submerge yourself in the island's turqoise water swimming next to the underwater ecosystem. Say hello to sea bass, sting rays and much more. After the swim, enjoy a flavorful,dominican bbq lunch on the sandy beach surrounded by palm trees. FYI: You have to be a swimmer!



One Word...Food



Age:Any

Price: $-$$$

Sazon!!! Need I say more? Dominican Republic is home to some of the best food in the world. It would be a crime to travel here and not try some of their staples: tres golpes, sancocho, mangu, tostones,pastelon,etc. I recommend going to street vendors for the traditional antojitos (snacks) or any inexpensive restuarant the plaza has to offer. If you are on the boujee side,there are plenty of upscale restaurants you can visit that will satisfy your palette with authentic and fusion cuisine.
