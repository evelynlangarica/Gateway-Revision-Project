About

La Romana is located in the tropical country of Dominican Republic. It is known for its beautful beaches including the famous "Saona Island" that has crystal clear water. The temperature does not go below 70 degrees year round. The rich culture of the Domincan people has added beauty to this city. Grab a presidente(DR's official beer), put on some bachata and geat ready to explore some of the great attractions the city has to offer.
